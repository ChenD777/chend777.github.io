About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Mechanical Engineering at the National University of Singapore (NUS). My research aims to develop deployable, trustworthy AI-based control frameworks for complex energy systems, with a particular focus on data center cooling.

Education

  • Ph.D., Aug 2022 – Apr 2026, National University of Singapore

  • B.S., Sep 2015 – Jun 2019, Northeast Forestry University

Research summary

I combine first-principles modeling with learning-based methods to improve prediction accuracy, interpretability, and control robustness. My recent work includes:

  • Adaptive Physically Consistent Neural Networks (APCNN) for thermal dynamics modeling
  • Physically Consistent Machine Leanring (PCML) based-MPC for energy optimization
  • Deep reinforcement learning (SAC/TD3/PPO) for hybrid-cooled data center control with safety awareness

Current topics

  • Physics-informed forecasting models for thermal dynamics
  • Robust & adaptive MPC for long-horizon energy optimization
  • Safe DRL with constraint handling and violation monitoring
