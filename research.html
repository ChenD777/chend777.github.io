Research

My research centers on energy-efficient and safe operation of data center cooling systems. I develop physics-informed learning models and advanced control methods that work under realistic system dynamics, uncertainties, and operational constraints.

Project 1: Hybrid machine learning-based prediction and intelligent control of data center thermal environment (Aug 2022 – Oct 2025)

  • Developed a hybrid physics-informed machine learning framework for data center thermal modeling, introducing Adaptive Physically Consistent Neural Networks (APCNNs) that achieve higher predictive accuracy with significantly reduced computational cost compared to conventional PCNNs.
  • Achieved ~20% lower MAE for short-term (15-minute) thermal forecasting and up to ~80% error reduction over multi-day horizons on real-world data center operational datasets, while rigorously preserving physical consistency through theoretical analysis.
  • Integrated APCNN-based prediction models into a physics-informed machine learning–assisted model predictive control (MPC) framework, enabling coordinated and adaptive optimization of data center cooling systems under both normal operation and previously unseen high-load conditions.
  • Extended the control framework to a long-horizon physics-informed adaptive MPC (PIML-AMPC), achieving approximately 20% cooling-energy reduction in short-term evaluations and ~24% annual energy savings compared with fixed-setpoint control strategies.
  • Enhanced robustness and deployment readiness by tightly coupling learning-based prediction with first-principles physical knowledge, ensuring stable temperature regulation and reliable operation under realistic disturbances and operational variability.

Project 2: Safe and Energy-Efficient Control of Data Center Cooling Systems via Deep Reinforcement Learning (Oct 2025 – Dec 2026)

  • Built an end-to-end DRL control platform for hybrid-cooled data centers with closed-loop training and evaluation.
  • Implemented and benchmarked SAC, TD3, and PPO, achieving ~15% energy savings while maintaining stable thermal performance.
  • Advanced safe DRL via constraint handling and violation monitoring to improve reliability under diverse conditions.
